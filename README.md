## Accepted Tokens

- ETH (Base mainnet)
- USDT (Base mainnet)
- USDC (Base mainnet)

## How It Works

1. Connect your wallet on [tipcryp.io](https://tipcryp.io)
2. Select your payment token
3. Send a minimum of $25
4. Your $TCM allocation is locked at Stage 1 price
5. Tokens are released automatically at TGE on January 1, 2027
